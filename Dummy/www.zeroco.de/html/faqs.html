How can i subscribe to ZeroCode?

You can visit our pricing page to know various subscription plans, choose the one that suits your purpose. Get in touch with our sales team to ease the subscription process.

Can I extend my free trial?

Yes, you can. Place a request for the extension of the free trial with a valid reason and email it to our customer support. Our team will analyze the need and act upon it as deemed necessary.

Can I place a service request from the starter plan?

Yes, you can request the ZeroCode premium services, but these are charged additionally to your starter plan.

How can i upgrade my subscription?

Login to your ZeroCode account and choose the upgrade option to select the package and place a request.

How the charges are adjusted if I upgrade my account within the current subscription period?

Till you upgrade, we continue to charge you as per your previous plan alone.

How can I try ZeroCode?

Your engagement with ZeroCode begins with a Trial on request. Please share a request to our sales team with the purpose and details.

Who can use ZeroCode? Do I need some technical background?

ZeroCode can be used by anyone with an intent to develop digital solutions. Technical background is not a necessity.

How can I learn ZeroCode?

You can learn by subscribing to various self-learning courses offered by ZeroCode academyor by participating in exclusive training programs conducted by the ZeroCode.

How can I reach ZeroCode?

You can send an email to ZeroCode with your requirement.

What can be built with ZeroCode?

ZeroCode can be used to build wide range of IT solutions across industries. Some of the use cases include internal IT systems, customer applications, process automation, data visulalization & live monitoring, legacy modernization etc. For more information visit the Solutions page.

Is my requirement can be built with ZeroCode?

You can refer our solutions section to see what can be built with ZeroCode. Additionally, you can post your requirement to our team for further assistance.

Can I build enterprise solutions with ZeroCode?

Yes, ZeroCode can be used to build enterprise solutions.

Can I build AI based solutions with ZeroCode?

No, We are currently not supporting the AI based app development, but we have added it in our pipeline.

Can I build highly secure financial apps with ZeroCode?

ZeroCode is built with the most of the security standards. Please reach us with your specific requirements and we will reach you back on the possibilities.

How can I rech you for support?

Drop an email to support, use our chatbot or request a callback.

How quickly will you get back to me?

You can expect the callback/email within 2 hrs. post request.

What types of issues are supported?

Technical support for any issues with using ZeroCode, Sales support for purchasing and upgrading ZeroCode, Account support to manage ZeroCode account.

How quickly will you fix my issue?

It is completly depends upon the issue and difficult to pre-estimate the time. We will fix them as soon as we can.

Do you speak my language?

The support is available in English.

How do I register for Training?

You can visit our training page to view the schedules and register accordingly. You can also drop an email to our support for training registration.

How much time does it take to get ZeroCode training?

We have various training programs ranging from 6 hrs. day to 100 hrs. Visit our training page further information or drop an email to our support for training information.

Do you offer Online training?

Yes, visit our academy for more details.

How many days can I access the course material post course completion?

You have 30 days access to your content post course completion. You can request for the extention by stating a valid reason.

Can I get my money back, if i fail to attend the course post registration?

No, please refer our refund polocies for further information.

Can i build my own custom reusable components in ZeroCode?

Yes, you can build and reuse the components across your application.

Can I connect to external databases?

You can connect to external databases via API services.

Can I write custom code?

Yes, you can write custom html, css and JS code and also write your own custom service calls and integrate it with ZeroCode.

Can I add my own UI theme?

Yes, you can import your theme files and create a new custom theme. Please refer the theme guidelines to build a theme.

Can I reuse my application?

You can export your app as a template and reuse it whenever necessary.

What are the service on offer?

You can request a service for UI/UX design and development, Custome coding development, Work/Project management, and Hire ZeroCode developers. For more information, visit our services page.

How can I hire ZeroCode developers?

Share your requirement and request ZeroCoders from your ZeroCode account management. We will reach you back once we receive the request.

Do you offer bespoke development?

Yes, post your requirements and we will reach you for further proceedings.

Can I access the services as part of my subscription?

Some of these services are offered as part of Enterprise plans, for the other plans, these services cost extra.

How much it costs to hire a ZeroCode developer?

Please contact sales for the cost and other information related to hiring ZeroCoders.
