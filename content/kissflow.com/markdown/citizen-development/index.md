## Frequently Asked Questions
#####  Can citizen development coexist with traditional IT-led digital transformation efforts? 
###### 
Yes, citizen development complements IT-led initiatives by distributing development efforts across business users while IT ensures governance and security. This collaborative approach accelerates digital transformation and helps organizations meet evolving business demands effectively. 
#####  How do organizations benefit from adopting citizen development? 
###### 
Organizations benefit by clearing IT backlogs, improving process efficiency, and fostering innovation. Citizen development allows business teams to address their needs independently while maintaining IT oversight, leading to faster time-to-market and enhanced collaboration. 
#####  What is citizen development, and how does it accelerate digital transformation? 
###### 
Citizen development enables non-IT employees to create custom applications and automate processes using no-code platforms. This approach accelerates digital transformation by reducing reliance on IT resources, empowering business users, and addressing operational challenges faster.
