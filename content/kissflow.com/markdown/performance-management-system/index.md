Kissflow’s Performance Management System (PMS) app facilitates this by mapping role-based skills and enabling talent managers to conduct annual, half-yearly, quarterly, or monthly feedback/appraisal processes. With self-rating, reporting manager rating and reviewing manager rating features, the app streamlines approval workflows and offers comprehensive performance metrics via the dashboard, ensuring clarity in objectives and efficient employee feedback processes.
  * **Performance reviews**


  *     * Conduct performance reviews and feedback sessions annually, half-yearly, quarterly, and monthly. Generate performance reviews in bulk for all employees, categorized by joining months, quarters, or selectively.
    * Track team-wise performance reviews in the dashboard for HR/talent managers and managers.


  * **Mapping technical skills/competencies**
    * Map technical skills and competencies based on roles and behavioral skills and competencies based on employee’s level/grade. 
    * Automatically fetch technical and behavioral skills in the Annual performance review based on employee's role and level/grade.


  * **Employee confirmation**
    * Manage employee confirmation processes, including feedback mechanisms. Track team-wise employee confirmations in the dashboard for HR/talent managers and managers.


  * **Performance improvement**
    * Implement performance improvement processes with actionable tasks. Track team-wise PIP in the dashboard for HR/talent managers and managers.


  * **Organization performance dashboard**
    * Create organization-wide employee performance summaries and dashboards for HR/Talent Heads.


  * **Employee profile**
    * Maintain employee profiles with quantitative data (e.g., ratings, promotion status) and qualitative data (e.g., manager feedback, upcoming goals).

## Frequently Asked Questions
#####  What is a performance management system, and how does it benefit organizations? 
A performance management system is a strategic approach that helps organizations monitor, evaluate, and enhance employee performance through goal-setting, continuous feedback, and regular reviews. Using a low-code platform like Kissflow, businesses can swiftly implement customized performance management workflows, facilitate real-time feedback, and ensure alignment between individual performance and organizational objectives. This leads to improved productivity and enhanced employee engagement, ultimately contributing to the organization’s success. 
#####  How can Kissflow’s low-code platform simplify the implementation of a performance management system? 
Kissflow's low-code platform enables organizations to build and customize their performance management systems without requiring extensive technical knowledge or complex coding. Users can easily create forms, define performance metrics, establish evaluation criteria, and automate feedback processes through an intuitive visual interface. This flexibility speeds up deployment, boosts user adoption rates, and enables businesses to adjust to changing performance management requirements quickly. 
#####  Can I integrate Kissflow’s performance management system with other business applications? 
Kissflow's performance management system can easily integrate with various business applications, improving overall functionality. Whether HR software, project management tools, or communication platforms, Kissflow enables easy data exchange and collaboration. This integration feature ensures that all pertinent performance data is accessible in one location, making informed decision-making and comprehensive performance tracking easier. 
#####  How does Kissflow support continuous feedback in the performance management process? 
Kissflow's performance management system focuses on continuous feedback, enabling managers and employees to share insights and evaluations regularly. The platform offers real-time feedback forms, check-ins, and performance journaling, facilitating ongoing discussions about performance and growth. This approach promotes transparency, supports development, and assists employees in staying aligned with their goals throughout the performance cycle. 
#####  Is it possible to track employee performance trends over time using Kissflow? 
Certainly! Kissflow's performance management system offers analytics and reporting features that enable organizations to monitor employee performance trends over time. Managers can utilize visual dashboards to consolidate performance data, recognize accomplishments, pinpoint areas for improvement, and display individual and team progress. This data-driven approach empowers businesses to make well-informed decisions regarding talent management, training requirements, and overall workforce development strategies.
